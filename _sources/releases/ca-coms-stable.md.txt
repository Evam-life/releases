## 1.3.2

2025-05-21

### Highlights

* **Fixed an issue with PTT when using a Motorola radio:** A bug was introduced in the previous version that could lead to the PTT signal being held on after answering an incoming Simplex call when using a Motorola radio, it should now be fixed.

## 1.3.0

2025-05-20

### Highlights

* **Added the option to automatically open the app upon accepting a Phone or TETRA call:** It is now possible to configure COMS to automatically open upon accepting a Phone call, a TETRA call, or both. The behavior can be customized as usual through the [COMS Configuration Tool](https://www.coms-configuration-tool.portal.evam.life/).
