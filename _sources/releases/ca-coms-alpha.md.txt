# Vehicle Services: COMS App changelog - Alpha

`````{warning}
You are viewing the Alpha releases of the COMS Certified App.
`````

## 1.4.0

2026-01-29

### Highlights

* **The app can now open automatically and show the Keypad page upon receiving a TETRA or Phone call:** When using the setting introduced in 1.3.0, COMS will now automatically open and display the keypad page upon receiving a call.

## 1.3.2

2025-05-21

### Highlights

* **Fixed an issue with PTT when using a Motorola radio:** A bug was introduced in the previous version that could lead to the PTT signal being held on after answering an incoming Simplex call when using a Motorola radio, it should now be fixed.

## 1.3.0

2025-05-20

### Highlights

* **Added a setting to automatically open the app upon accepting a Phone or TETRA call:** It is now possible to configure COMS to automatically open upon accepting a Phone call, a TETRA call, or both. The behavior can be customized as usual through the [COMS Configuration Tool](https://www.coms-configuration-tool.portal.evam.life/).
