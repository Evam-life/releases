## 1.1.5

2025-05-20

### Highlights

* **Added the option to automatically open the app upon accepting a Phone or TETRA call:** It is now possible to configure COMS to automatically open upon accepting a Phone call, a TETRA call, or both. You can configure this in the General Tab.
